Screening A Broadway-Style Musical Show

“THE REAL LOVE: A New Musical” is coming to Canada, starting with Vancouver.

Featuring Tony winners Daisy Eagan and Cady Huffman, directed by LA Drama Critics Circle Award winner Chris Shelton, and inspired by moving poetry of Supreme Master Ching Hai, world-renowned spiritual leader, humanitarian and artist, this magical show boasts an exceptional cast, mesmerizing orchestra, and original compositions by legends like Oscar & 5-time Emmy winner Bill Conti.

What's more? Event Tickets and Vegan Feast are FREE with a limited number of seats.

Believe Only In Love

The musical is inspired by a true story about a woman who is deeply in love with her equally tender husband, a doctor. But as their happy marriage blossoms, a calling propels her to make a heartrending decision – remain with her beloved or sacrifice everything in search of happiness for all humanity.

In what seems a destined journey, she embarks on travels through India and the Himalayas, encountering adventure, danger, and surprises along the way.

The unforgettable story is sure to bring tears, laughter, and poignant memories for audiences of all backgrounds.
